Cyber Law Response

  1. Do not pay deposits, training fees, equipment fees, or verification charges.
  2. Preserve screenshots, email headers, phone numbers, account details, UPI IDs, and URLs.
  3. In India, report cyber fraud through cybercrime.gov.in or your local cyber cell.
  4. Inform the real company if its name, logo, or employee identity is being misused.

Project objective

AI-Based Fake Job Offer Detector

This project helps job seekers identify fake employment offers by combining text risk analysis, network security checks, cryptographic hashing, and cyber law awareness.

Cyber law context

Reporting and evidence

The app is India-focused for academic use. It references the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal and IT Act concepts such as identity theft and cheating by personation.
